How Billing Works

We keep the payment process simple and predictable.

  • Step 1: Pay the $497 setup fee to kick off the project.
  • Step 2: We build a demo site for your approval.
  • Step 3: Pay the build fee once you're happy with the demo.
  • Step 4: We set up hosting and launch your site on your custom domain.
  • Step 5: Your first month of hosting is free. Billing begins automatically after 30 days.

A 6-month minimum commitment applies to all hosting & care plans. Billing authorization is collected at handover.
