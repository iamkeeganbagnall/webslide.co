Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Send us a message and we'll get back to you.

How long does it take to build my website?

Most websites are ready for review within 2 weeks of receiving your setup fee and any content you provide. The timeline depends on how quickly you can provide text, images, and feedback on the demo.

Do I need to provide content, or do you write it for me?

We ask that you provide the core content — information about your business, services, contact details, and any photos. We can help clean up and format text, but the words should represent your business in your voice.

What happens if I need more than 10 pages?

No problem! Additional pages beyond your package can be added for $347 per page. Just let us know during our initial call and we'll quote accordingly.

Can I see the website before it goes live?

Absolutely. We build a demo site for you to review and approve before anything is published to your domain. We only go live once you're happy with the result and the final invoice is paid.

What does the $97/month hosting plan include?

Your monthly plan includes fast managed hosting, an SSL certificate, security monitoring, automatic backups, quarterly performance reviews, and minor content updates. Your first month is free.

Do I own my website?

Yes. Once the project is paid in full, the website belongs to you. We hold the hosting arrangement, but the design and content are yours.

What if I want to cancel hosting?

We ask for a 6-month minimum commitment on all hosting and care plans. After that, you can cancel at any time with 15 days notice before your next billing date.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Yes — every site we build is fully responsive and tested on phones, tablets, and desktops. This is included in every package at no extra cost.

Do you help with domain names?

We can advise on choosing a domain name and help you point it to your new site, but domain registration itself is handled through a registrar of your choice (like Google Domains or Namecheap).

Still have questions?

We're happy to answer anything. Reach out and we'll get back to you within one business day.
