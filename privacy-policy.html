Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Webslide ("Webslide", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards personal information when you visit webslide.co, contact us, or purchase our website development and related services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number, business details, website URL, and any information you submit through our Google Form, contact form, emails, or other communications.
  • Payment information — payments are processed by a third-party payment processor (Stripe). We do not store your full payment card details.
  • Basic technical data — like IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited that may be collected through standard server logs to help keep our website secure and functioning.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond and provide services — to reply to your inquiry, prepare a quote, onboard you, and deliver website development, hosting, maintenance, upgrades, and related services.
  • To manage billing — to process payments, send invoices or receipts, and manage subscriptions (where applicable).
  • To improve and protect — to troubleshoot issues, prevent fraud/abuse, and improve the reliability and security of our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations — where required by applicable laws or regulations.

3. Communications

We do not send marketing emails or SMS campaigns. We may contact you for service-related communications such as onboarding, project updates, billing, and support.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We do not intentionally use advertising pixels or marketing trackers. Our website may use essential cookies or similar technologies required for basic functionality, and our hosting provider may collect standard access logs.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate our business, such as:

  • Service providers — such as payment processors (Stripe) and tools we use to deliver services (e.g., hosting, domain/DNS tools, email, file transfer, or project collaboration tools).
  • Legal requirements — where required to comply with law, enforce our agreements, or protect our rights and safety.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, including to provide services, maintain records, and comply with legal obligations.

8. Your Rights (Canada / PIPEDA)

If you are in Canada, we comply with applicable privacy laws including the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). You may request access to or correction of your personal information, or withdraw consent where permitted by law.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party websites or tools. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before providing personal information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
